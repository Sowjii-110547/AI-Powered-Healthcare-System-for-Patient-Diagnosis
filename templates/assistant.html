Recently Asked Questions

What are the symptoms of heart disease?
Common symptoms include chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue, dizziness, and irregular heartbeat. Early detection is crucial.
How accurate are kidney disease predictions?
Our AI model achieves 95%+ accuracy based on laboratory values and clinical parameters for reliable kidney function assessment.
Can I get diabetes risk assessment without all lab values?
Yes! Our system provides predictions based on available information. More data points improve accuracy but aren't required.
What blood tests are needed for kidney assessment?
Key markers include creatinine, BUN, electrolytes (sodium, potassium), hemoglobin, and eGFR for comprehensive kidney evaluation.
How often should I get heart disease screening?
Annual screening is recommended for adults over 40, or earlier if you have risk factors like family history or high blood pressure.
